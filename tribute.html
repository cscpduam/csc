In Memory of Our Beloved Colleague

Our Department mourns the untimely loss of Mr. Subhomoy Dey, a respected faculty member whose dedication, knowledge, and kindness touched the lives of students and colleagues alike. In his memory, a souvenir was published to celebrate his life and contributions to the Computer Science Department. We would like to extend our heartfelt thanks to our most hardworking student, Ritumoni Banikya, for his outstanding effort and dedication in designing and serving as the editor of our souvenir. Also thanks to Kripa Rabha and CSc--2022-24 batch. Your hard work and creativity have truly made this project special.
