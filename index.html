Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide quality education in Computer Science that empowers students with technical skills, ethical values, and innovative thinking to meet global challenges while preserving local cultural identity.

Vision

To become a center of excellence in computer education and research in Northeast India, producing socially responsible professionals who contribute to technological advancement in Assam and beyond.

SWOC Analysis

Strengths

  • Only Department in Goalpara offering B.Sc. CS Honours
  • All faculty positions filled with qualified staff
  • Excellent undergraduate curriculum (GU CBCS)
  • Well-furnished lab with 13 PCs + server
  • Cooperative environment and collaboration

Weaknesses

  • No Internet/LAN facilities
  • No trained lab technician
  • No PG/PhD programs
  • No dedicated department block
  • Need additional computer lab

Opportunities

  • Build departmental block with advanced labs
  • Collaborate with other institutions
  • Increase enrollment in higher studies
  • Promote computer literacy in region

Challenges

  • Implement campus WiFi for ICT education
  • Subscribe to scientific journals
  • Motivate students to choose CS careers
  • Secure research funding
